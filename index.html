Each skill has up to 100 levels, and they all start at level 0 with 0 experience.

Your first level will be up to level 1.

Each level has its own experience bar, and all skills have the same amount of total experience. Currently, the only benefit of leveling up is greater access to rare items or more recipes for each industry.

Experience increases through your character's actions, for example, for farming, you need to harvest crops.

AI NPCs are able to respond to player actions in a more natural way, and their behavior can evolve depending on scenarios and interactions with players. The implementation of such technologies requires the active intervention of developers and users. Everyone will adjust AI settings to ensure balanced gameplay, preventing interactions from being too random or overly difficult. Developers will also be responsible for optimizing performance and ensuring the ethical use of artificial intelligence in a gaming context.

An anti-cheat system using artificial intelligence provides more effective means of detecting and preventing cheating in games. AI algorithms analyze player data in real time, identifying suspicious behavior patterns such as overly precise actions or unusual movements that could indicate cheating. Anti-cheat will also improve itself, update algorithms in response to new methods of fraud, ensuring the constant evolution of the anti-cheat system. It is important that the anti-cheat works fairly and does not affect honest players. The developers are closely monitoring the reaction of the gaming community, providing feedback and making adjustments to the anti-cheat system to ensure an optimal gaming experience.
